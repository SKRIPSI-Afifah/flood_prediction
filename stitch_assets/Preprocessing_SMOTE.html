What is SMOTE?

Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique is a statistical technique for increasing the number of cases in your dataset in a balanced way.

Instead of just duplicating existing samples, SMOTE generates synthetic examples by selecting similar records and interpolating between them. This prevents the model from simply memorizing the minority class.
